SAKHI

Sakhi (2000) is a Telugu romantic drama film directed by Mani Ratnam. The movie follows the story of two friends, Rukmini (Keerthi Reddy) and Surya (Aryan Rajesh), who grow up together and eventually fall in love. However, their social differences and family opposition threaten to tear them apart. The film explores themes of love, friendship, and social class, set against the backdrop of a small town in Andhra Pradesh. The movie features stunning cinematography, memorable performances, and a soulful soundtrack by A. R. Rahman. Sakhi received critical acclaim for its sensitive portrayal of complex relationships and its nuanced exploration of social issues. The film is considered a classic of Telugu cinema and is still widely regarded for its beautiful storytelling and memorable characters. If you enjoy romantic dramas with depth and substance, Sakhi is definitely worth watching.
